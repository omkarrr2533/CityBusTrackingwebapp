Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the bus tracking?
Our GPS tracking system provides real-time location updates with an accuracy of within 50 meters. Updates are refreshed every 30 seconds.
Is there a mobile app available?
Yes! Our mobile app is available for both Android and iOS devices. You can download it from Google Play Store or Apple App Store.
What should I do if a bus doesn't arrive?
If a bus is delayed or doesn't arrive, please contact our customer support immediately. We'll help track the bus and provide alternative options.
